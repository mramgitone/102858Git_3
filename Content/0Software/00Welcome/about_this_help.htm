Notification Standards

To conform to standards for CE, IEC 82079-1 and ISO/IEC Guide 37:2012, this help file also uses a number of notifications to represent safety and other related information. For example, Notes, Cautions and Warnings follow these requirements:

  • Notes relate to the last point or section covered in the text.
  • Cautions represent a danger to the product and/or related machinery only.
  • Warnings represent a danger to the person, product and/or related machinery.
