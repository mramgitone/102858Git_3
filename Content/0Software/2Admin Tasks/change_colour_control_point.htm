Change the Colour Control Point & Highlight Colour
  1. Select the inside of the colour bar. A hover gesture will appear if you move the mouse to the coloured bar in the control point. The colour selection dialog will appear.

The Colour Selection Dialog

  1. Choose the required colour. Another hover gesture appears that you can drag across the colour selection dialog, or if you want to be more precise, drag the arrow in the grade scale. You will also see the Shot and Waterfall displays change to match. When you are happy with the colour click anywhere in the Colour Map dialog to make the popup go away.

  2. If you want to dismiss the colour selection dialog, place the pointer anywhere outside the dialog.
